Raila Amolo Odinga

Kenyan statesman – Profile & Timeline

Born: 7 January 1945

Birthplace: Maseno, Kisumu District, Kenya

Raila Amolo Odinga was a Kenyan politician and statesman who played a major role in shaping the country's modern political landscape. He served as Prime Minister from 2008 to 2013 and became widely known for his leadership in democratic reforms.

Key Roles
  • Prime Minister of Kenya (2008–2013)
  • Leader of the Orange Democratic Movement (ODM)
  • Opposition leader & democracy advocate
